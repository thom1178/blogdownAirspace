About

"Hiding within those mounds of data is knowledge that could change the life of a patient, or change the world." - Atul Butte, Stanford

I'm Jamel. This blog is about statistics, R, data science and pretty graphs. Sometimes I get interested in a particular topic, and can't get the thing out of my head until I've done some digging. Many of the things on this site is authored by me, but I reference those items that are not. You can find me on linkedin, github, and twitter. The latter is not so much career based, but has funny memes.

methodolgy

The beautiful thing about data analysis is that a lot of the time, there is not a single right answer. A model I build could be different than one you build. Therefore, the methodolgy is the most important aspect to any analysis. In order for me to understand your results, I have to know how you got there. This is how I got here.
